    Fortnite Season X - Out of Time

      Introduction: Fortnite is a free 100 player battle royale type shooter game. It was released on July 21st 2017 and it uses the Unreal Engine for its game. The developer is called Epig Games.

      Game Modes: Fortnite has two main game modes called 'Save the World' and 'Battle Royale". Originally, the game is a zombie tower defense game but later, in three months, the developers created a Battle Royale game mode. This increased its popularity almost immediately.

      Cosmetics and Gameplay: Fortnite does not only include action packed battle royale games, but it also includes a well-thought storyline with amazing and thrilling live events that you can experience in-game. There are also tons of cosmetics that you can get or buy in the game's store or in the battle pass.

    Moonlighter

      Introduction: Moonlighter is an action RPG game with fantastic artwork and it requires certain strategic thinking but you can also just enjoy the artwork. It was realeased on May 29th 2018 and the game's developer is Digital Sun, publisher is 11 Bit Studios. In Moonlighter, you take over a shop your father used to own and you run it while fighting monsters in the dungeons to get antiques to sell.

      Between Dimensions DLC: This DLC adds an extra gate to the already-existing five gates and that gate leads to a mysterious dungeon that has a green-ish look to it. To know more (includes spoilers), go to: http://moonlighterthegame.com/

      Interactions: You can interact with pets and talk to people around the village to understand them and know the trend or news in the village. The more you progress, the more you unlock throughout the game.

     

 

Minecraft

  •

    Above are photos of Minecraft

    Minecraft (PC edition, Pocket edition, Xbox edition, Playstation edition)

      Introduction: Minecraft is a well-known sandbox video game, where you can battle monsters and explore different biomes. Its developers are Mojang and Microsoft Studios and it was released on November 18th 2011. In Minecraft, you can build, explore and fight, for example, you can explore the randomly generated desert temples, you can build floating mansions the size of mountains or you can fight mobs and creatures that you may encounter during your expeditions around the world.

      Mods: In Minecraft, you can import Mods, which modify your gameplay or your world to create a different environment, create a game within Minecraft or more whacky things. There are also mods that Mojang make themselves and those are great to use.

Bloons Tower Defense Battles' website: https://ninjakiwi.com/Games/Tower-Defense/Bloons-TD-Battles.html

    Above are pictures of BTD Battles in-game and its logo

    Bloons Tower Defense Battles - BTD Battles

      Introduction: BTD Battles is a free Tower Defense game with Monkeys popping balloons. It was released on April 20th 2016 and its developer is Ninja Kiwi. It is a fun and competitive game at the same time. It can make you think of many different strategies to defeat your opponents and lead yourself to victory.

      Competition and Clubs: You can join clubs to get exclusive custom maps or you can join competitions to win medallions, which are the currency used in-game or club tickets to access clubs for free.

      Strategies: There are tons of ways to play a game in BTD Battles and each way envokes you to think of the different type of balloons' weaknesses and strengths. You can also send balloons to your opponent, so you will want to send balloons that your opponent can't defend to get a quick victory or you can stretch the game and see how long you can last!
