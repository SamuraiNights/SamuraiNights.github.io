    Strings, Integers and Conditions with a Website in the Mix

      Strings: Strings are words or phrases that are not variables or integers. They are words and numbers you want to show in the final program and are contained in quotation marks.

      Integers: Integers are numbers that usually can't be shown unless you convert the integers(s) to strings. Integers are numbers like these: 1234567890 and they can be put into variables or to be calculated.

      Conditions: Conditions are these: if, else if or else. They help set a circumstance and help you with creating an answer to the circumstances.
